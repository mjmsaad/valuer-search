Things to Keep in Mind

  • Commission: Our seller's commission is 15% (including GST), which will be deducted from your final payout.
  • Packaging: It is your responsibility to ensure wine is packed safely, particularly during hot weather. Wickman's is not liable for damage caused by improper packaging or handling, even when a recommended courier is used.
  • Bottle Condition: Please do not send bottles that are weeping, leaking, have low fill levels, or have not been discussed with us beforehand.
  • Ownership: By consigning your wine, you confirm that you hold clear title to the bottles, free from any liens or claims.
